FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe? ▾
Yes. All data is stored in your personal Firebase Firestore database. Security rules ensure only you — authenticated with your UID — can read or write your own data. We never sell or share your data.
Does it work offline? ▾
Yes. FitOS enables Firestore offline persistence. You can log workouts and food even without internet. When you reconnect, everything syncs automatically to Firestore.
Is there an Indian food database? ▾
Absolutely. FitOS has 100+ Indian food items including dal tadka, roti, paneer, biryani, idli, sambar, poha, and many more — all with accurate macro data. Perfect for Indian athletes.
How is the workout plan generated? ▾
During 13-step onboarding, you provide your goals, fitness level, equipment, injuries, and schedule. FitOS uses this to select the optimal split (Full Body, Upper/Lower, or PPL) and generates a complete periodized program stored in Firestore.
Can I use it on multiple devices? ▾
Yes. Because everything is stored in Firebase, you can log in from any device and your data is always up to date. Real-time Firestore listeners update your dashboard automatically across tabs and devices.
What if I cancel? ▾
Your account and free tier features remain active. You can export all your data anytime. We never delete your Firestore data — it belongs to you.
