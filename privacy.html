Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

FitOS collects information you voluntarily provide: name, email address, fitness profile data (age, weight, height, goals), workout logs, nutrition entries, recovery check-ins, and Coach Center interactions. This data is stored in your personal Firebase Firestore database.

2. Firebase & Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by security rules that ensure only you — authenticated with your unique Firebase UID — can access your data. Firebase Analytics is used to understand aggregate usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to power FitOS features: generating workout plans, tracking nutrition, providing recovery scores, and Coach Center guidance. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

4. Data Ownership

You own your data. You can export a complete copy at any time from the Export page. You can delete your account and all associated data permanently from the Account page. Deletion is irreversible.

5. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Firestore security rules enforce per-user data isolation. Passwords are managed by Firebase Authentication and are never stored in plain text. We recommend using a strong, unique password and enabling Google Sign-In for additional security.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at our contact page.
